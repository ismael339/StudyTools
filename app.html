Frequently Asked Questions

What types of text can I use?
You can paste textbook excerpts, lecture notes, articles, essays, Wikipedia pages, or any study material. The longer and more detailed the text, the better the summary, outline and test will be. Aim for at least 3-4 paragraphs for best results.
How does the AI generate the practice test?
The AI reads your text, identifies the most important concepts and facts, then generates multiple-choice questions that test your understanding of that specific material. Each question comes with an explanation so you understand why the correct answer is right.
Is my text sent to a server?
Your text is sent to the AI model to generate the study materials, but it is not stored or saved on any server after processing. Each session is independent and nothing is retained once you close the tab.
Can I use this for any subject?
Yes — the tool works for any subject: biology, history, economics, literature, chemistry, law, psychology and more. It reads whatever you paste and generates materials specific to that content.
How many times can I use it per day?
The tool is free to use with no strict daily limit. Use it as many times as you need for your studying sessions.
