Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

StudyTools is built to help students use free study tools with as little friction as possible. This page explains the basics in simple language.

Information we collect

We may collect basic analytics data such as page visits, device type, browser, country-level location and pages used. This helps us understand what tools are useful and what needs improvement.

AI tool input

When you use an AI feature, the text you submit is sent to our AI provider through our API so the tool can generate a response. Do not paste private passwords, personal documents, payment details or sensitive information.

Local storage

Some tools may save recent work in your browser, such as recent study sessions or tool history. This is stored locally on your device and can usually be cleared by clearing browser data.

Cookies and analytics

StudyTools may use analytics services to measure traffic and improve SEO, usability and performance. We removed intrusive external ad scripts while the site focuses on growth and trust.

Contact

If you need a privacy request handled, contact the site owner through the public project or domain contact method available for StudyTools.pro.

Student safety

StudyTools is for learning support. Students should use AI for explanations, hints, practice and organization, not to submit work they do not understand.
