Frequently Asked Questions

How does this word counter work?
The counter analyzes your text in real time as you type or paste. Words are counted by splitting on whitespace, characters include all typed symbols, sentences are detected by punctuation (. ! ?), and paragraphs by double line breaks. Reading time is estimated at 200 words per minute (average adult reader).
Does it count characters with or without spaces?
Both. The "Characters" stat includes spaces and all punctuation. The "No Spaces" stat counts only non-space characters, which is useful for platforms like Twitter or character-limited fields.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is estimated at 200 words per minute, which is the average reading speed for an adult. Speaking time uses 130 words per minute, which is the average speaking pace. These are estimates — your actual speed may vary.
Is my text saved anywhere?
No. Everything happens entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server or stored anywhere. When you close the tab, your text is gone.
What are common essay word counts?
A standard paragraph is 100–200 words. A short essay is 500–800 words. A typical college essay is 500–650 words. A 5-paragraph essay is around 500–1000 words. A research paper is usually 1500–5000 words depending on the assignment.
