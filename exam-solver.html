Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects does this tool support?
The solver works for virtually any academic subject: math (algebra, calculus, geometry), sciences (biology, chemistry, physics), humanities (history, literature, philosophy), economics, and more. Just paste the question and select the subject for more targeted explanations.
How accurate are the explanations?
The solver provides detailed step-by-step explanations based on established academic knowledge. For factual and conceptual questions it is very reliable. For complex calculations, always verify the final answer. Use it as a learning tool to understand the process, not just get the answer.
Can I use this for homework help?
Yes — the tool is designed to help you understand concepts, not just copy answers. The step-by-step format explains the reasoning behind each answer, which helps you actually learn the material and perform better on your own exams.
Is there a limit to how many questions I can ask?
The tool is free to use with generous daily limits. There's no account required and no paywall. Just paste your question and get an explanation instantly.
